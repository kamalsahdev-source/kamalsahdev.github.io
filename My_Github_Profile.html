Strategy

I'm a senior technology and business leader with 25+ years driving data strategy, digital transformation, and cloud and platform modernization across financial services. My career spans JP Morgan Chase, Citi, First Data, and USAA — each chapter defined by building platforms that move business outcomes, not just advising on them.

At USAA, I innovated and built both the Bank Marketing Growth Engine and the Data & Analytics platform — an integrated system that drove $100MM+ in annualized revenue. I've led Customer 360 initiatives deploying 500+ Snowflake data assets, launched GenAI and RAG-powered conversational analytics in production, and directed Open Banking data strategy at scale.

Today I'm applying that enterprise foundation to emerging AI capabilities: prototyping agentic AI workflows for complex financial services problems, developing intelligent investment portfolio tools, and advising on the path from legacy financial systems to cloud-native, AI-enabled architectures.

The most valuable thing a senior data leader can do right now is understand AI deeply enough to build with it — not just manage teams that do. My conviction: organizations that don't adopt and evolve with AI quickly will be left behind before they realize it — and the leaders who will matter most are those who can bridge the strategic and the technical.
